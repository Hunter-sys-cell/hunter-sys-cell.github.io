The Books Of The Holy Bible

The Holy Bible is broken up into two parts, The Old Testament and The New Testament.
Of these two parts, The Holy Bible contains 73 books in its complete collection.
There are 46 books in the Old Testament section of The Holy Bible.
There are 27 books in the New Testament section of The Holy Bible.
